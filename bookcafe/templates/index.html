Why Use TheBookCafe?

There are a number of websites available in market to buy and sell goods and to provide booking services to the customers. And the apps for related websites are also available online. But there is no website available which is particularly concerned with the selling and buying of second hand books. There is no website available which is totally dedicated to helping books, notes and other study materials find new homes. The various existing websites have different consequences which are described below.

Varieties of books

There will be different categories and genres of books. Also there are notes and other study materials for different examinations.

Environmental friendly

As the books will be reused hence there will be less wastage of papers.Thus our website is completely environmental friendly.

Reduced Cost

As there will be no shipping charges and no taxes the cost will be reduced.The customers have to pay only seller's demand.

Only books

This website is entirely based on reselling of old books so that the one who needs it can get it at low cost.
